# Dealer Kiosk System - Gearmax Pro

**Gearmax Pro Dealer Kiosk System** is the third implementation of the kiosk system, developed specifically for the Gearmax Pro brand within the H7X group. This version focuses on capturing distributors for high-performance equipment and accessories, with tailored forms to evaluate experience in selling specialized products, inventory capacity, and geographic distribution reach.

## Main Features

- Form optimized for specialized accessories distributors
- Inventory and logistics capacity assessment
- Geographic territory coverage mapping
- Scoring system for distributor prioritization
- Integration with inventory management system
- Real-time dashboard of applications during trade shows

### Key Responsibilities and Achievements:

- **Developed the third iteration of the kiosk system** adapted for Gearmax Pro products
- **Implemented an advanced scoring system** that prioritizes distributors by sales potential
- **Created a geographic mapping module** to visualize prospects' territorial coverage
- **Integrated with inventory systems** to evaluate distributors' logistical capacity
- **Designed a real-time dashboard** displaying capture metrics during events
- **Optimized performance for intensive use** during peak hours at trade shows
