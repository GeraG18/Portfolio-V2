# Cord Application

**Cord** is an innovative project developed by Córdova Industrial's Innovation and Technology Development area (CIDTcor), aimed at transforming business automation through Industry 4.0 solutions.

Using cutting-edge IoT technology, Cord enables companies to optimize their manufacturing operations through a comprehensive management application. The platform offers real-time monitoring of critical production variables, remote control of connected devices, and a customized 24/7 alert system to ensure operational continuity.

## Main Features

- Monitoring of PLC equipment in industrial zones
- Customized alerts on key parameters of each device
- Remote control of equipment from anywhere in the world
- Equipment segmentation by zones for better organization

### Key Responsibilities and Achievements:

- **Led the complete UX/UI design**, creating intuitive and accessible interfaces for all users using Figma
- **Developed the functional prototype** by implementing the design in Ionic with Angular and Tailwind CSS, establishing the technical foundations of the product
- **Created the visual identity and branding** used in app stores (PlayStore and AppStore), ensuring professional market presence
- **Configured and optimized Google Cloud infrastructure** for iOS/iPadOS, implementing critical services like Cloud Messaging and Crashlytics
- **Implemented the secure authentication system** integrating multiple providers (Google, Apple, and Email) with specific configurations for iOS/iPadOS
- **Managed the complete publication process** on AppStore, taking the application from development to public availability
