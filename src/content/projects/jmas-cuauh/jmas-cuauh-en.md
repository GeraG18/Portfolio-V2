# JMAS - Cuauhtémoc

**JMAS Water Management** is a specialized system developed by Córdova Industrial Integradores (CIDTcor) for the Municipal Water and Sanitation Board of Cuauhtémoc, designed for centralized control and monitoring of water wells across various city zones. This restricted-access application allows JMAS operators to manage water resources more efficiently and effectively, representing a complete renovation of the previous solution to address critical performance, coding, and functionality issues.

## Main Features

- Centralized control of municipal water wells
- Real-time monitoring of water levels and quality
- Customized notification system based on specific conditions
- Interface optimized for efficient operational management
- Restricted access exclusive to authorized JMAS personnel

### Key Responsibilities and Achievements:

- **Contributed to the complete application redesign** identifying and proposing solutions for critical performance and coding issues in the previous system
- **Designed and developed the UX/UI structure** creating intuitive interfaces for water resources management
- **Structured and optimized database components** for efficient handling of well monitoring data
- **Implemented advanced logic in Node-RED** developing systems for data retrieval and upload, plus customized notification functions based on specific conditions
