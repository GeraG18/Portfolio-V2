# New InteliHogar Application

**InteliHogar** is an innovative project developed by Córdova Industrial's Innovation and Technology Development area (CIDTcor), designed to provide a complete smart home ecosystem accessible with just a few clicks. The application allows users to control and manage all their smart devices installed at home, offering advanced features such as room-based organization, complete device control, and an invitation system for sharing specific control with other users.

## Main Features

- Centralized control of home smart devices
- Organization by rooms and zones
- Invitation system with granular permissions
- Complete IoT ecosystem management
- Intuitive interface for quick setup

### Key Responsibilities and Achievements:

- **Designed and developed the UX/UI structure** creating intuitive interfaces for smart device management
- **Structured and optimized the database** for efficient handling of device data and configurations
- **Implemented the multi-factor authentication system** integrating Google, Apple, and Email providers with specific configurations for iOS/iPadOS
- **Developed the complete visual identity** and branding used in official app stores
- **Collaborated on data transmission implementation** between the application and home IoT devices
- **Optimized and refactored existing code** significantly improving application performance
- **Participated in developing a smart alarm system** integrated with home devices
- **Managed the complete publication process** on AppStore for iOS and iPadOS platforms
