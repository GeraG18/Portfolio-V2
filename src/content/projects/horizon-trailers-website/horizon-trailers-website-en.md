# Horizon Trailers Website

**Horizon Trailers Website** is the main corporate website developed for Horizon Trailers, a leading manufacturer in the trailer industry and part of the H7X corporate group. This comprehensive platform presents the complete range of utility, commercial, and specialized trailers, offering business and individual customers a modern digital experience to explore products, request quotes, and access technical resources.

## Main Features

- Complete trailer catalog with detailed technical specifications
- Integrated online quotation system
- Multimedia gallery of products and real applications
- Resource section for customers and distributors
- Responsive design optimized for all devices
- Integration with CRM and business management systems

### Key Responsibilities and Achievements:

- **Led the complete UX/UI design** in Figma, creating an intuitive and visually appealing information architecture
- **Developed the full implementation in Next.js** using React and TypeScript with scalable architecture
- **Implemented advanced SEO and SSR strategies** optimizing performance and search engine visibility
- **Optimized performance by 80%** reducing initial load times to less than 1.5 seconds
- **Significantly improved accessibility** complying with WCAG 2.1 AA standards for digital inclusivity
- **Established proactive security protocols** implementing regular version reviews and data encryption
