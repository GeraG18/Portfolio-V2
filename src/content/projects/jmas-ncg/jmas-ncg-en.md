# JMAS - Nuevo Casas Grandes

**JMAS Water Management** is a specialized system developed by CÃ³rdova Industrial Integradores (CIDTcor) for the Municipal Water and Sanitation Board of Nuevo Casas Grandes, designed for centralized control and monitoring of water wells across various city zones. This restricted-access application, specifically adapted for the needs of Nuevo Casas Grandes, allows JMAS operators to manage local water resources more efficiently and effectively, representing a complete renovation of the previous solution to address critical performance, coding, and functionality issues.

## Main Features

- Centralized control of municipal water wells in Nuevo Casas Grandes
- Real-time monitoring adapted to local hydrological characteristics
- Customized notification system based on specific regional conditions
- Interface optimized for efficient operational management of the local system
- Restricted access exclusive to authorized JMAS personnel of Nuevo Casas Grandes

### Key Responsibilities and Achievements:

- **Contributed to the complete application redesign** identifying and proposing solutions for critical performance and coding issues in the previous system
- **Designed and developed the UX/UI structure** creating intuitive interfaces adapted to the specific water management needs of Nuevo Casas Grandes
- **Structured and optimized database components** for efficient handling of well monitoring data from the region
- **Implemented advanced logic in Node-RED** developing systems for data retrieval and upload, plus customized notification functions based on specific local conditions
