# RanchBoss Website

**RanchBoss Website** is the online store and informational platform developed for RanchBoss, a specialist manufacturer of livestock trailers and rural operation equipment within the H7X group. This site combines e-commerce functionality with educational content, offering ranchers, farmers, and ranch owners a comprehensive solution to acquire specialized equipment while accessing resources on best practices in livestock management.

## Main Features

- Complete catalog of livestock trailers and rural equipment
- E-commerce system with agricultural financing options
- Resource library on livestock management and animal safety
- Trailer configurator with options specific to different livestock types
- Blog section with tips for rural operations
- Integration with logistics systems for shipments to rural areas

### Key Responsibilities and Achievements:

- **Designed dual e-commerce/informational experience** balancing sales with educational value
- **Implemented specialized configurator** for livestock trailers with options by animal type
- **Developed integrations with financing systems** specific to the agricultural sector
- **Optimized for rural connections** with advanced compression and deferred resource loading
- **Implemented intelligent recommendation system** based on type of livestock operation
- **Created modular architecture** allowing future expansion to international market
