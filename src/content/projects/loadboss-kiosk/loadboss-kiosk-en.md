# Dealer Kiosk System - LoadBoss

**LoadBoss Dealer Kiosk System** is a specialized adaptation of the kiosk system to capture applications from distributors specializing in heavy-duty truck beds and flatbeds. Developed for commercial trade shows in the heavy transport sector, this version includes specific fields for experience with loading equipment, types of vehicles serviced, and installation capabilities, optimizing the recruitment of qualified distributors for LoadBoss products.

## Main Features

- Specialized form for heavy equipment distributors
- Specific fields for truck and flatbed experience
- Validation of technical capabilities and certifications
- Automatic pre-qualification system based on experience
- Integration with existing distributor database
- Specific metric reports for the transport sector

### Key Responsibilities and Achievements:

- **Adapted existing kiosk platform** for specific requirements of the heavy transport sector
- **Designed specialized forms** that capture relevant LoadBoss equipment experience
- **Implemented pre-qualification system** that automatically evaluates distributor suitability
- **Integrated with existing dealer database** to avoid duplicates and enrich data
- **Developed specific reporting modules** for distributor market analysis
- **Optimized interface for rapid use** considering visitor flow at industrial trade shows
