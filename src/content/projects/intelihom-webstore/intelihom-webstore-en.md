# InteliHom Web Store

**InteliHom Web Store** is the internationalized e-commerce platform developed for the US market expansion of Córdova Industrial Integradores' (CIDTcor) smart home ecosystem. This online store, specifically adapted for American consumers, presents the complete catalog of products for creating smart homes, allows contracting maintenance and 24/7 monitoring subscriptions, and manages the entire purchase process with completely localized interfaces and content for the target audience.

## Main Features

- Completely internationalized platform in English
- Product catalog adapted for the US market
- Subscription system localized for US norms and regulations
- Purchase process optimized for international consumers
- Specific content and marketing for the target market

### Key Responsibilities and Achievements:

- **Implemented the internationalization system (i18n)** in Angular configuring all infrastructure for multilingual support
- **Completed the full platform translation** adapting all texts, interfaces, and content to English for the US audience
- **Managed production deployment** implementing and configuring the store specifically for the target international market
