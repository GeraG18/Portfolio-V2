# Enegrin Application

**Enegrin** is an innovative project developed by Córdova Industrial's Innovation and Technology Development area (CIDTcor), designed to provide Mexican production companies with access to green energy with higher quality and stability, preventing interruptions in their production processes. The application offers contractors a comprehensive monitoring platform with detailed information about energy generation system performance, including real-time charts, performance data, and an interface to send commands to control devices when necessary.

## Main Features

- Real-time monitoring of green energy systems
- Charts and visualizations of energy performance data
- Remote control of IoT devices and PLCs
- Interface for sending commands to control equipment
- Alert and notification system for system status

### Key Responsibilities and Achievements:

- **Designed the preliminary UX/UI structure** in Figma, creating intuitive interfaces before final branding development
- **Structured and optimized the database** in Firestore for efficient handling of energy monitoring data
- **Implemented the multi-factor authentication system** integrating Google, Apple, and Email providers with specific configurations for iOS/iPadOS
- **Developed the visual identity and branding** used in app stores (PlayStore and AppStore)
- **Collaborated on data transmission implementation** between the application and industrial control devices (PLC and IoT)
- **Managed the publication process** on AppStore for iOS and iPadOS platforms
