# InteliHom Backend

**InteliHom Backend** is the internationalized version of the administration system developed for the US market expansion of Córdova Industrial Integradores' (CIDTcor) smart home platform. This system, derived from InteliHogar, provides all necessary administrative functionalities to manage user locations, connected devices, online store products, and other ecosystem operations, specifically adapted for the needs and regulations of the US market.

## Main Features

- Completely internationalized system for US English
- Administrative management adapted for the international market
- All components translated and localized
- Infrastructure prepared for global expansion
- Maintenance of original backend functionalities

### Key Responsibilities and Achievements:

- **Implemented the internationalization system (i18n)** in Angular configuring the complete infrastructure for multilingual support
- **Completed the full backend translation** adapting all texts, labels, and messages to English for the US audience
- **Managed production deployment** implementing and configuring the system specifically for the target international market
