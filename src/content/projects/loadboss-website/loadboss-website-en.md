# LoadBoss Website

**LoadBoss Website** is the digital platform developed for LoadBoss, a specialist in manufacturing heavy-duty truck beds and flatbeds within the H7X corporate group. This website is specifically designed for the B2B market, offering carriers and logistics companies detailed technical information, product configurators, and tools to calculate load capacities and custom specifications.

## Main Features

- Interactive configurator for truck beds and flatbeds
- Load capacity and weight distribution calculators
- Technical library with specifications and installation manuals
- Quotation system for custom configurations
- Project gallery and case studies
- Integration with fleet management systems

### Key Responsibilities and Achievements:

- **Designed specialized user experience** focused on technical needs of the heavy transport sector
- **Implemented interactive product configurator** allowing real-time customization of platforms
- **Developed advanced technical calculators** for load capacities and structural analysis
- **Optimized performance for heavy data** maintaining response times under 1.5 seconds
- **Implemented advanced security measures** to protect technical and quotation data
- **Created scalable architecture** prepared for future integrations with industrial ERP systems
