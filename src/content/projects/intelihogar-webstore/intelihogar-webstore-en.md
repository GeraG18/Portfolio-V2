# InteliHogar Web Store

**InteliHogar Web Store** is the e-commerce platform developed for Córdova Industrial Integradores' (CIDTcor) smart home ecosystem, designed to facilitate users' complete acquisition of home automation systems. The online store presents the complete catalog of available products for creating smart homes, allows contracting maintenance and 24/7 monitoring subscriptions, and manages the entire purchase and installation process, offering an integrated experience from product selection to ongoing support.

## Main Features

- Complete catalog of smart home products
- Subscription system for maintenance and 24/7 monitoring
- Integrated purchase process with multiple payment options
- Complete management of orders and contracted services
- Responsive design optimized for desktop and mobile

### Key Responsibilities and Achievements:

- **Implemented the new business model** integrating subscription payments and device purchases in a unified platform
- **Integrated the Stripe payment system** implementing all transaction processing and security functionalities
- **Designed and developed the desktop interface** creating an optimized shopping experience for computer users
- **Developed calculation algorithms** for subscription systems, including monthly payment projections and financial analyses
